Server Rules

Guidelines:

Always Be Civil:
Be civil. Don’t post anything that a reasonable person would consider offensive, rude or abusive.
Respect each other. Just treat everyone with respect, and you shall receive so back.
Disagreements and directed comments are allowed but remember to criticize ideas, not people. Personal attacks won't be tolerated. Instead, provide reasoned counter-arguments that improve the conversation.

If You See a Problem, Report It
When you see bad behavior, don’t reply. It encourages the bad behavior by acknowledging it, consumes your energy, and wastes everyone’s time. Just report it for a moderator to look into and take action if necessary.

Rules:

  • 1 No Spam / Advertising:
    Advertising other Discord servers, Twitch channels & YouTube channels/videos is not allowed and is considered as spam. You may post links if a moderator gives you permission to do so. #twitch_and_youtube can be used for advertising your youtube video or your ongoing livestream if you have the Twitch and/or the YouTube role (you can apply for that). You may post game information or updates in form of links or videos.
  • 2 Flaming:
    Any material which constitutes defamation, harassment, abuse or slander, towards staff or users, is strictly prohibited.
  • 3 Offensive Content:
    Material that is sexually or otherwise obscene, racist, or otherwise overly discriminatory is not permitted on this server. Use common sense.
  • 4 Illegal Content:
    Providing or asking for information on how to illegally obtain or provide software or music is forbidden.
  • 5 Trolling / Disturbing:
    Trolling people using the text/voice rooms is not allowed, same goes for disturbing.
