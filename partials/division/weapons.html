Weapons

The weapon arsenal in The Division covers from pistols to powerful calibers like the assault rifle and marksman rifle sector.

Handling and Inventory

You can carry and use up to 3 weapons simultaneously, consisting of a primary and secondary weapon plus one sidearm (pistol).

You still have an inventory where you can have more weapons and gear that you can swap to. You will even have a stash at the Base of Opperation.

All Weapons

All weapons are characterized by the parameters of damage, precision, range, recoil and the number of mod slots, which can be changed and improved through appropriate extensions and attachments.

Modifications

Mod Slots

Weapons can be equipped with modifications that are nothing else than items. The number of modifications that can be attached is based on the number of mod slots the weapon has, which in turn depends on the weapon’s archetype, item level and rarity level.

Beginning with classic silencers, to different types of ammunition, such as incendiary ammunition to magazine extensions, the modifications ensure that you can build the perfect weapon of your choice. The following modification types are available and also separated into sub-categories:

  • Handle (partially confirmed)
  • Underbarrel (Short/Long)
  • Magazine
  • Muzzle (Small/Large)
  • Optic (Short/Long)

Because mods are handled as items, their quality is also based on a rarity level and item level and can have up to 2 random bonuses. Noticeable is that silencers will always have a threat reduction bonus and can therefore only get 1 other random bonus.

Skin Slot

In the fight rather meaningless, but for many players of great value are weapon skins. An additional mod slot allows you to apply either a color or a whole skin pattern to your gun.

Weapon Talents

Weapon talents are unlockables that allow you to customize your whole build even more. Although these unlockable abilities have nothing to do with the character’s abilities at first, they work similar to talents. They get activated when a special action is triggered. Examples are

  • Critical damage boost on headshot
  • Regain health on critical hit
  • Medkit used » increased damage for a short time

and there are a bunch more of them. They make also use of the 3 main attributes and can sometimes depend on them.
