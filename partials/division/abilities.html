Abilities

Skills

You can equip 2 base skills and 1 signature skill. Base skills can have 1 of 3 modifications and 1 master (passive) modification.

Medical

(*) - Signature Skill | MWU - Medical Wing Upgrade
Name Description Required MWU
Pulse Sends out recon pulse that marks hostiles and allies through walls and covers. -
First Aid Deploys a device that heals all allies within a small radius. -
Support Station Drops a support station that heals allies and can be used to revive downed allies. Virus Lab
Recovery Link* Heals and cures nearby allies. Revives downed allies within range. Can “overheal” beyond maximum health. Disaster Aid

Tech

(*) - Signature Skill | TWU - Tech Wing Upgrade
Name Description Required TWU
Sticky Bomb Deploys a bomb that sticks to most surfaces and explodes when detonated remotely. -
Turret Deploys a stationary turret that attacks nearby hostiles until it is destroyed or its battery runs out. Control Room
Seeker Mine Deploys an automated mine that seeks out hostile targets before exploding on proximity. Communications
Tactical Link* Increases damage and critical hit chance to a user and all nearby allies. Division Tech

Security

(*) - Signature Skill | SWU - Security Wing Upgrade
Name Description Required SWU
Ballistic Shield Equips a portal Ballistic Shield that absorbs damage. You can only use sidearm while the shield is active. -
Smart Cover Reinforces a cover, increasing the damage and decreasing the incoming damage for all allies behind it. Situation Room
Mobile Cover Deploys a temporary cover for one person, which provides standard cover protection until destroyed. Armory
Survivor Link* Increases damage resistance and speed for the users and nearby allies. Barracks

Talents

You can equip up to 4 talents. Talent slots unlocked by leveling up (or by a perk).
