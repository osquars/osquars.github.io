Abilities

The ability system in The Division compensates for the nonexistent character classes and is divided into the three main sections skills, talents and perks.

Skills

  • You can equip 2 base skills and 1 signature skill.
  • Base skills can have 1 of 3 modifications and 1 master (passive) modification.
  • You unlock skills and mods by upgrading each wing in the Base of Operation.
