Mission Items

What is Mission Items?

I call it Mission Items since it is only available in the missions and are not saved between missions.

There are three types of Mission Items

  • Collect Items
  • Consumable Items
  • Replenish Items

Helpful hint

Using the 'T' (default keyboard button) is very helpful to notify your team members about possible dangers or items.

Collect Items

Collect items will give the entire party upgraded dice in the Loot Roll

Tomes

  • Tomes are in the main missions and take up the same slot as Medical Supplies/Draught.
  • Tomes can be found in certain places in the mission
  • There are 3 pieces per mission.
  • Tomes gives you more EXP after the mission.

Grimoire

  • Grimoires are in the main missons and take up the same slot as the Buff Potion.
  • Grimoires are hidden somewhere in the main missions.
  • Everyone in the group loses 33% of max health for each Grimoire.
  • Replace the Grimoire and you get back 33% of max health. You can not pick up the grimoire again.
  • There are 2 pieces per mission.
  • Grimoire gives you more EXP after the mission.

Cursed Die

  • Cursed Die can be found in chests that are here and there in the missions.
  • There are up to 2 pieces per mission.
  • Cursed Die gives you more EXP after the mission.
  • Can be found on the sack-rat sometimes.*

Consumable Items

This Items have slots in the UI under the weapons on the right side and beside the Characters in the bottom.

Medical Supplies/Draught

  • Medical Supplies used to heal you or your allies.
  • Draught can only heal you.
  • Medical Supplies heals up to 90% of max health.
  • Draught heals a fixed number amount (around 30%).
  • Can be found in chests and on the ground everywhere.
  • Can be found on the sack-rat sometimes.*

Buff Potion

  • Speed Potion make you move and attack quicker.
  • Damage Potion make you attack harder.
  • Can be found in chests and on the ground everywhere.
  • Can be found on the sack-rat sometimes.*

Grenades/Bombs

  • Grenades damages in a small area and are good for bigger enemy
  • Fire Bombs create an area of fire that will make fire damage to enemy
  • Can be found in chests and on the ground everywhere.
  • Can be found on the sack-rat sometimes.*

Replenish Items

Infinite Ammo Crates

  • Infinite Ammo Crates will give you infinite amount of supplies.
  • Can be found on certain places in the missions.

Ammunition Supplies

  • Ammunition Supplies will give you a full resupply once.
  • Can be found in chests and on the ground everywhere.
  • Can be found on the sack-rat sometimes.*

* Sometimes you stumble across an Skaven holding a sack over his back. If you kill him before he can run away, you can get Items from him. We can call him Sack-rat
