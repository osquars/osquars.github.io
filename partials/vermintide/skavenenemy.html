Skaven - The Enemy

The Skaven are the primary enemies encountered throughout the course of Vermintide. We will engage this enemies in variable amounts through all the missions. Here we list them and give you some basic information about them. I always want to help find better tips and hints to handle these skavens in better ways.

Clanrat

  • HP: Low
  • Strengths: They are lurking on the roof, attacking from all directions.
  • Weaknesses: Weak vs everything
  • Description: The Clanratsâ€™ strength comes from their great numbers, surrounding and overwhelming their enemy by attacking from all directions.
  • Tactic / Hints: Use aoe attacks to kill when swarming
  • Lore: Read

Skavenslaves

  • HP: Low
  • Strengths: They swarm, when the horn is blowing
  • Weaknesses: Weak vs everything
  • Description: The Skavenslaves will always attack in great numbers, either through a coordinated attack signalled by a battle horn, or through an ambush.
  • Tactic / Hints: Use aoe attacks to kill when swarming
  • Lore: Read

Stormvermin

  • HP: Medium
  • Strengths: Hard hitting, Armored on the body, Able to knockback players
  • Weaknesses: Headshots, Armor Piercing
  • Description: Wearing heavy armor, red cloth and a crest adorned helmet, the Stormvermin are among the fighting elite of the Skaven clans.
  • Tactic / Hints: Can be killed with 1 headshot from all ranged weapons with headshot spacial
  • Lore: Read or Read (Stormvermin Patrol)

Poison Wind Globadier

  • HP: Medium
  • Strengths: Shoots from a distance, Cloud attack that does dmg and obscures vision, Kamikaze attack when low on hp
  • Weaknesses: Slow, Low dmg output
  • Description: Upon impact, the globe shatters and spreads the toxic fumes, dazing and damaging any who might be caught in the resulting cloud.
  • Tactic / Hints: Kill it before it charges in and self explodes, and avoid its poison cloud
  • Lore: Read

Gutter Runner

  • HP: Low
  • Strenghts: Very fast, Pounces on player and disables, Teleports away if not killed, Strong vs lone players
  • Weaknesses: Easily killed if team stays together
  • Description: For a Gutter Runner, stealth is everything. Always moving in the shadows, the Gutter Runner wear a black cloak to blend in with their surroundings and make as little noise as possible.
  • Tactic / Hints: Whisper noises will often hint of nearby assassin rats. Always be on the move will make it harder for him to jump you.
  • Lore: Read

Packmaster

  • HP: Low
  • Strenghts: Disables player, Moves player further away from group once grappled, Strong vs lone players
  • Weaknesses: Does low dmg, Easily killed if team stays together
  • Description: Without support from the rest of the group, the hero will face unavoidable death when caught by a Packmaster.
  • Tactic / Hints: Kill it before it can get to you with ranged weapon.
  • Lore: Read

Rat Ogre

  • HP: Very High
  • Strenghts: Jump attack, High dmg, Knockback
  • Weaknesses: Bombs, Bombs and more Bombs.
  • Description: On the whole, the Rat Ogre is a mindless brute, relying on aggression and muscle to get the job done. It will focus its attacks on one hero at a time.
  • Tactic / Hints: Use bombs and range weapon when possible then switch to melee weapon when being targeted and use block to negate most dmg when getting hit, then move away to recover stamina and reengage.
  • Lore: Read

Ratling Gunner

  • HP: Medium
  • Strenghts: Strong single target ranged attack
  • Weaknesses: Easily flanked, wind-up time, takes long to switch target
  • Description: The Ratling Gun is a six-barreled monstrosity of a gun, powered by warp steam and operated with a hand crank.
  • Tactic / Hints: Kill it before it start shooting, or take it down when its focused on another player
  • Lore: Read

Sack Rat

  • HP: Low
  • Strenghts: None
  • Weaknesses: Carries around loot and have no weapons
  • Description: Rat with a bag - Kill it to get random loot and sometimes cursed die.
  • Tactic / Hints: Kill it fast or it'll run away
  • Lore: Read(dev blog)
