Red Moon Inn

The Red Moon Inn. The home for you and your party.
An Innkeeper that have nothing else to do then telling you that you should join the “party” instead of hiding in your room.
You can take a beer, have a chat or just trash the place.

The Red Moon Inn is your home. Where you have your own room that is decorated with stuff from your character. From here you operate your mission and gather your party before going to the mission. Get to know a little bit more about the innkeeper story here.

The Map

The Map is where you can set up your party, select your mission and search for party. Only the one created a party (Host) can interact with the map if you are in party already.

  • You/Host(if in a party) can start missions
  • Choose mission and set difficulty
  • Advance settings like distance, hero filter, hosting and/or auto ready
  • See if friends are online to invite or join
  • Set the mission to private

Ammo Crate

Why not have some extra ammo when you have a party. Perfect if you want to test your new weapon or just want to shoot something in the Inn.

Inventory

Here you can go and change weapons and trinkets for your character.

Forge

In our lovely home the Red Moon Inn we have a Forge. On this forge we can make stuff, upgrade, mould our items or destroy them.

On the Forge upgrading cost and salvage collect "shards". Every type of shards represents the rarity of the items.

  • White (Plentiful)
  • Green (Common)
  • Blue (Rare)
  • Orange (Exotic)
  • Red (Veteran)

Fuse

  • Takes 5 items of the same rarity and fuse them to a new and better one.
  • You can only fuse items of the same rarity.
  • Fuse will have 20% probability for each weapon you add.
    • Example 1: 5 Common Great Hammers will give you 1 Rare Great Hammer
    • Example 2: 1 Common Crossbow, 2 Common Great Hammers and 2 Common Longbows will give you a (20%, 40%, 40% chance) Rare item.

Upgrade

  • Takes one common or higher item and unlock one passive trait.
  • Upgrading weapons by unlocking traits becomes more expensive as you upgrade your weapon.
  • You can however choose freely which trait you want to get as the 1st by selecting that trait.
    • Common traits require Plentiful shards
    • Rare traits require Common shards
    • Exotic traits require Rare shards

Salvage

Salvage every item you click on and make it to 1-3 shards of the same rarity.

You can only get items by the Loot Roll or level up your account.

Shrine of Solace

Comes with the free DLC (Sigmar’s Blessing), It might be bigger post about the shrine later on.

Pray

  • Use Pray on the Shrine to get a Weapon for your character by selecting what type of weapon (Melee, Range or random), rarity and the hero trinket.
  • Select the Hero Trinket for the Character you want an Item for.
  • Pay with shards from that rarity you have selected and you will get your Item.
