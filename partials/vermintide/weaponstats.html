Weapon Stats

We all want to kill skaven but we want to do it in our way to satisfy our self. To understand what weapon is best for you I have here something of a list for you to look on and see what can be a good weapon for you to use on your favorite character.

Weapon Stats

Damage

  • The higher bar you have the easier it is the kill with one blow.
  • Clan rats kills with one blow from around 1/2 of the bar (Various from mission difficulty).
  • Depending on rarity of the weapon you will get a higher damage boost. Damage boost varies from weapon types.

Attack speed

  • Hard to set a number on this.
  • Max is like almost instant while in the bottom we have maybe up to 2 sec.

Targets (how many targets you can hit)

  • See it as a cone in front of you, the higher bar you have the bigger the the cone are. Max is like 90 degree from you.
  • If you like to see with numbers I have split it up in 6 zones:
    • 1 target
    • 1-2 targets
    • 2-3 targets
    • 3-4 targets
    • 4-5 targets
    • 5-7 targets

Knockback (pushback on hits)

  • The Higher you have the more push/stun on the enemy you get on a hit.
  • Low bar is likely not to push/stun at all

Range

  • The distance is from just in front of you to almost the other side of a square (Thinking of the big square with the Sigmar statue in the magnus mission)

Special

Some weapons have special passive characteristics.

  • Armor penetration - Can penetrate heavy armor
  • Head shot - High damage when hitting the head
  • Burn - Small damage over time
  • Poison - Small damage over time

Stamina

  • All melee weapons have their stamina for blocking and pushing.
  • Push off cost two Stamina (one shield icon)
  • Higher mission difficulty makes blocking and pushing more important to use.

Traits

  • Passive traits that can be unlocked on the Forge (Red Moon Inn thread) from common and higher rarity weapons
  • Depending on rarity of the weapon will give you different amount of trait slots.
    • Common (1 Trait slot)
    • Rare (2 Trait slots)
    • Exotic (3 Trait slots)
  • List of traits can be found at my weapon and trinket traits thread.

Bright Wizard

Brun them all. If you are not afraid of a little fire, Bright Wizard is a character for you. With the balance of fire and death you as at Bright Wizard will do a lot of damage. Bright Wizard is a special character and have unique mechanics. Staff is the weapon with most variations of attacks. Every one is unique and can be from single enemy to Multi enemies and Area of effect.

(*) Starter weapon.
